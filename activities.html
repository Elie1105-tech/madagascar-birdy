Rainforest Birding Adventures

Venture into Madagascar's lush eastern rainforests, home to iconic species like the velvet asity, helmet vanga, and Madagascar serpent eagle. These tours combine intensive birding with the magic of pristine jungle environments.

Highlights:

  • Andasibe-Mantadia and Ranomafana parks
  • Ground-rollers and mesites tracking
  • Lemur and chameleon encounters
  • Waterfalls and natural pools
  • Sustainable eco-lodge accommodations

Western Dry Forest Birding

Explore the unique dry deciduous forests of western Madagascar. This ecosystem harbors specialized species adapted to seasonal drought, including couas, vangas, and the critically endangered Madagascar fish eagle.

What to expect:

  • Kirindy and Ankarafantsika reserves
  • Crested coua and sickle-billed vanga
  • Baobab landscapes at sunset
  • Fossa and nocturnal wildlife spotting
  • Tsingy limestone formations

Coastal & Wetland Birding

Discover Madagascar's aquatic bird life along pristine coastlines and freshwater wetlands. Encounter migratory shorebirds alongside Madagascar's endemic waterbirds in mangroves, lagoons, and lake systems.

Key species:

  • Madagascar sacred ibis and pond heron
  • Sakalava rail and white-throated rail
  • Madagascar fish eagle territories
  • Coastal forest birding opportunities
  • Marine wildlife observation
