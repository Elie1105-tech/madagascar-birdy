Your Local Madagascar Wildlife Experts

With over 280 bird species and more than 100 endemics found nowhere else on Earth, Madagascar is a paradise for serious birdwatchers and wildlife photographers. As a locally-owned and operated agency based in Ambatolampy Antehiroka, we bring decades of firsthand knowledge of Madagascar's hidden birding hotspots and pristine ecosystems.

Our specialized birdwatching tours focus on rare endemic species including vangas, ground-rollers, mesites, and couas. We combine expert local guidance with sustainable eco-tourism practices, ensuring authentic wildlife encounters while supporting local communities and conservation efforts across Madagascar's diverse habitats.
