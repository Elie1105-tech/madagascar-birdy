About Madagascar Birdy

Madagascar is home to over 280 bird species, with more than 100 found nowhere else on Earth. As a local, independent tourism agency, we specialize in connecting international birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts with this unique island's extraordinary avian life.

Based in Antananarivo, we bring decades of local knowledge and a deep commitment to sustainable eco-tourism. Our tours are designed for serious birders, wildlife photographers, and anyone who appreciates the natural world.

Local Expertise
